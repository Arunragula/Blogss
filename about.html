About DotComDaily

DotComDaily is a personal blog — a running dot of honest, everyday thoughts on technology, life, business, and culture. No fluff, no clickbait, just observations worth sitting with for a minute.

Why this exists

Most feeds are optimized to keep you scrolling. This one is optimized for the opposite — a short, considered post now and then, written because it needed to be said, not because a content calendar demanded it.

Who's behind it

DotComDaily is written and maintained by an independent writer and developer who also builds the site itself — a small, self-hosted project.

What you'll find here

  • Short essays and opinion pieces on tech and everyday life
  • Occasional product recommendations for tools worth your time
  • No sponsored posts disguised as opinions
